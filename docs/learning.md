# Learning

OSIPI offers additional resources for continuous learning. 

Video tutorials on the basics of ASL and advanced ASL concepts are available [here](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLTIlnXdb9rwhoO8afyba3GQSh8ueShJ03&si=rTJUBVXftFicno3j).

A collection of recommended literature is available for [ASL](aslliterature.md), [DSC](dscliterature.md), and [DCE](dceliterature.md).

Basic principles of MRI acquisition, processing, and clinical application, as well as advanced lectures on quantitative MRI are available
at the YouTube channel of the TACTIX project: [TACTIX MRI Tutorials](https://www.youtube.com/@TACTIXTwinningProject/playlists).
